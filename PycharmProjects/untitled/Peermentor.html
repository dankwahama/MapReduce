ELIGIBILITY

  • Ghanaian Status
    • Applicants must be Ghanaians by birth or naturalization living in Ghana.
    • Non- Ghanaian applicant must be living in Ghana as of the mentorship period.
  • Age
    • Applicant must be 18 years or older.
  • Academic Standing
    • Applicant must have a CGPA of 3.0 or above from an accredited institution
  • Applicant must as of the start of the mentorship program be enrolled in an accredited tertiary Institution to be part of the program.

    • CRITERIA

  • Applicant must have intention of furthering their education to a higher level.
  • Applicant must be willing to disclose any personal information that is critical to this exercise to their mentors. This will give mentors more insights about the applicants and thus, help in designing topics or areas of discussion that suit specific needs.
  • Applicant must be an active person when it comes to responding to emails or communicating with their mentors.
  • Applicant should be willing to have at least one Skype Call/Meet up with their mentor each month to review tasks and discuss pressing issues concerning the mentee.
  • Applicants must be willing to serve as mentors after a specified number of years upon completion of their mentorship program.
